Woodland Charm

A regular destination for newlyweds, and families looking to enjoy the beauty of Cook Forest - from late nights at the campfire, to the changing of the leaves, to the wildlife everywhere.

Still At The Woods will be your relaxing escape. There are also countless outdoor adventures that are just minutes away. Canoeing, kayaking, hiking, horseback riding, and fishing are just some of the ways you can enjoy our little corner of the world. Visits to local wineries and breweries should not be missed. You also may want to include on your ‘to do’ list visits to our many local restaurants to enjoy live music outdoors during the summer months.

Ways to Stay

Reserving the cabin isn’t the only way to in the woods, we also have tent spots and RV spots available.

The Cabin

Featuring a charming full-size cabin with two bedrooms, eat-in kitchen including a dishwasher, laundry room, sunroom with a ping pong table, television with Blu-ray/DVA/VCR and stereo systems/radios throughout. Our beautiful cabin can sleep up to ten people.

Tent Spots

Separate from our cabin, we have 15 tenting spots with fire pits and picnic tables. Camp solo or camp with your family and friends. We want you to have fun. Firewood is provided and additional firewood is available for purchase.

RV

Have a camper? We have 10 spaces with electric that can be rented for the season, year round or for a fantastic weekend to enjoy the campgrounds and a steak from one of the local butcher shops over your campfire.
